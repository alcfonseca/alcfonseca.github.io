UI/UX, branding
2018/1

For my capstone project in university, I decided to bring one of my favorite books into the digital realm, using resources such as animation and interactions as a way to rekindle interest of younger populations.

Final Prototype ‚∏∫

Prepare and Research ‚∏∫

Since this was a capstone project, I spent a whole semester researching and reading about the target user-base, the current market and similar projects. When this was ready, I devised a methodology and began developing the site.

Figuring It Out ‚∏∫

Based on all the information gathered, a userflow was created and features fleshed out. With this in hand a preliminary wireframe was made which was later tested with members of the user base.

All Together Now ‚∏∫

With test results in hand, animations and interactions were planned out and the branding and final prototype created. The Ô¨Ånal result was tested once more with users and achieved positive feedback!

Final Reflections ‚∏∫
