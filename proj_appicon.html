Creating a Polished App Icon ⸺

Branding

2020

Product. SAP for Me is the online portal for SAP customers, providing a transparent view of their entire product portfolio and granting a personalized experience.

Goals. The project was starting to develop designs for a future iOS app and wanted an icon that would identify it as part of SAP but also as something new and modern. The challenge was making it modern yet identifiable as part of the SAP app suite.

Figuring It Out ⸺

Research. First step was to analyse if SAP had a common theme that governed all app icon designs. No guidelines were found, but a common theme between some of the apps was apparent. Based on that, initial designs were sketched out in a process named Braindumping to create as many ideas as possible.

Iteration. Soon after digital explorations started in Sketch. Special attention was given to brand identity. Issues were seen with some decorative elements because of the size of the icon and were tweaked, until a final five options were presented to the CPO, Business Owner and fellow teammates to decide the final version.
