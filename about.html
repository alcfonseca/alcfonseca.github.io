Welcome to my humble digital abode!

I'm Ana, a UX designer from Brazil working in the south of Germany. Since I was a child, drawing, gaming and reading have been steadfast passions of mine. In school I'd always design the t-shirts and posters to whatever events were happening long before I knew what the word design even meant. Soon enough, I was studying Visual Design for my bachelor's degree. At some point my love for the internet got the better of me and I got sucked into Web Design, which later turned into a career in UX Design. After 8 years working in the area, I’m excited to see how new technologies will impact the field.

Anyway, I now live in the south of Germany and eat Prezels. Alongside my normal hobbies like reading, watercolor, gaming and learning the acoustic guitar, I also have many granny-type hobbies, such as crochet, sewing, baking and gardening.
