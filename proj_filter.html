Upgrading the Filtering feature ⸺

UX/UI

2020

Product. SAP for Me is the online portal for SAP customers, providing a transparent view of their entire product portfolio and granting a personalized experience.

Goals and Challenges. There were various forms of simple filtering in most of the cards in the platform, but for users that deal with support incidents all day, a more robust solution was necessary. Thus a new filter feature was added to the card’s header where several different variables can be filtered simultaneously. Our challenge was to have it seamlessly fit into the card designs and go along with the other simple filtering.

Figuring It Out ⸺

Process. After an initial analysis and discussion of the requirements, research was done into the existing filters in the platform as well as on external sites. Balsamiq was used for an initial mockup to verify with teammates if all was well and soon after the concept was finalized in Sketch and handed-off in Zeplin and Invision.

User Testing. We were unsure if users would be able to find and use the complex filtering system, and for this reason we decided to test it. We were glad to see that it was straightforward for the testers (5 sucesses, 2 with assist) and that none of them thought the filtering was too complex.

Results ⸺

We now have a design for a complex filtering system which, although developed for a support topic, can be easily adapted for other situations at SAP for Me.
