SAP for Me is the online portal for SAP customers, providing a transparent view of their entire product portfolio and granting a personalized experience.

Both of the features came in the form of a business requirement from a Product Owner of one of the various teams in SAP for Me.

Filtering â¸º

There were various forms of simple filtering in most of the cards in the platform, but for users that deal with support incidents all day a more robust solution was necessary. Thus a new filter feature was added to the cardâ€™s header where several different variables can be filtered simultaneously.

Process. After an initial analysis and discussion of the requirements, research was done into the existing filters in the platform as well as on external sites. Balsamiq was used for an initial mockup to verify with teammates if all was well and soon after the concept was finalized in Sketch and handed-off in Zeplin and Invision.

Invoicing â¸º

As the platform grows, there is a ever present need to integrate other SAP systems and sites. Invoicing is a card where users can check on bills associated to their business, performing actions such as seeing due dates, statuses and paying. Because of its nature, it was placed in the Finance & Legal dashboard of SAP for Me but can be access through other cards.

Process. The source of the Invoicing card was analysed to figure out what information and functionalities had to be moved. Various explorations were done in Sketch and discussed with the team that owned the tool until it was finalized and handed-off through Zeplin and Invision.
