Crumbly Yogurt Cake

A fluffly yet crumbly cake recipe handed down to me from my mother.

Batter

  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup natural yogurt

Preheat oven to 180C. Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a bowl. In another bowl, mix the butter till creamy, then add in the sugar while continuously mixing. Slowly add in the eggs, egg whites and vanilla extract, mixing as each ingredient is added. Alternate between adding in part of the flour mixture and the natural yogurt till the ingredients are well incorporated. Pour batter into a lined and greased baking tin and prepare crumbly "Farofa". After batter is topped with the crumbly "Farofa", bake for 35 min or until toothpick comes back clean. Let cool and enjoy!

Crumbly "Farofa"

  • 50g butter
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt

In a small pan, cook butter at medium heat till it starts to brown. Set it aside to cool. In a bowl mix all of the other ingredients, then add them to the browned butter and mix until crumbly "Farofa" forms. Sprinkle it on batter before placing into the oven.
