SAP for Me is the online portal for SAP customers, providing a transparent view of their entire product portfolio and granting a personalized experience.

As the platform grows, there is an ever present need to integrate other systems and sites into SAP for Me. Invoicing is a card where users can check on bills associated to their business, performing actions such as seeing due dates, statuses and paying. Because of its nature, it was placed in the Finance & Legal dashboard of SAP for Me but can be accessed through other cards.

Figuring It Out â¸º

Process. First step was analysing Biller Direct (BD), the original tool from where the invoicing feature was coming from. The original information architecture and functionalities were studied, and the needs of the current users of the tool discussed. One of the biggest challenges was to accommodate business wants from the BD team with the needs of the everyday user of the system.

Various explorations were done in Sketch with feedback from the Biller Direct team until the design was finalized and handed-off through Zeplin and Invision. The card is currently under development.
